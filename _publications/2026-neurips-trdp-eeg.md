**Venue:** *NeurIPS 2026 Workshop* · Under Review

**Key results:**
- 5 Temporal Windows · 100–700 ms
- Peak Accuracy **98.49%**
- EEG-to-Image CNN · Perception vs. Imagination

A time-resolved decoding framework for distinguishing **Perception vs. Imagination** of visual objects from EEG. Converts EEG channels into 2D time–channel images for lightweight CNN classification across five overlapping windows. Achieves up to **98.49% mean accuracy** (200–700 ms), substantially outperforming ML/DL baselines — revealing that discriminative neural signatures emerge strongly **after 200 ms post-stimulus**.

**Authors:** **Thien Nhan Vo**, Yen Nhi Tran Thi, Ngan Nguyen Xuan Phuong, Xuan-The Tran

**Venue:** *arXiv* · [arXiv:2606.26506 [eess.SP]](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.26506) · 2026

**Links:** [[arXiv]](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.26506) [[PDF]](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.26506)

A lightweight CNN combining residual learning with Squeeze-and-Excitation (SE) attention for four-class EEG classification (normal/deprived × eyes-open/closed). Achieves **83.69%** subject-wise mean accuracy on a 35-subject dataset, outperforming the strongest baseline (Random Forest, 47.66%) by **+36.03 pp**, using only 5 frontal–occipital EEG channels and an image-based 2D convolution representation.

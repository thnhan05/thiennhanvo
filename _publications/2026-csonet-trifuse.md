**Venue:** *CSoNet 2026* · 15th International Conference on Computational Data and Social Networks  
Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam · November 16–18, 2026

A reliability-aware trimodal fusion framework for disaster damage assessment, combining heterogeneous data sources with adaptive weighting based on per-modality confidence estimates. Designed to remain robust under real-world sensing conditions where individual modalities may be noisy or unavailable.

I am an undergraduate researcher in **Robotics and Artificial Intelligence** at **HUTECH University**, with a strong mathematical foundation highlighted by multiple awards in the **Vietnam Student Mathematics Olympiad** (including a Gold Medal in Calculus and Silver/Bronze Medals in Algebra). I am affiliated with the **Information Security Laboratory (InSec Lab)** at UIT, VNU-HCM.

Driven by a passion for bridging theoretical mathematics with applied AI, my research focuses on **Large Language Models (LLMs) & Agentic Workflows** for smart contract security (LLM-augmented fuzzing), **privacy-preserving AI**, and **Brain–Computer Interfaces (BCI)**. My contributions to neural decoding and EEG intent recognition have been accepted at venues like **IEEE ICCE 2026** and are under review at **NeurIPS 2026 Workshops**.

Deeply committed to the global academic community, I actively engage in educational outreach and academic service. I have served as an Instructor for the **IMO Camp at National Taiwan Normal University** under MIT mentorship, as well as on the **Judging Committee for the ICPC Asia Taichung Regional Contest 2025**. My portfolio also includes interdisciplinary projects in precision agriculture and renewable energy systems, reflecting my commitment to high-impact, real-world engineering solutions.
